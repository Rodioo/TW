No #3. Money, Explained
Money, Explained has a similar vibe to the rest of the Explained series, using animation, celebrity narrators and expert interviews to give a good encapsulation to a complex concept.
No #2. Lupin
“Lupin” is as patient as it is smart, and its plot twists like melted railroad tracks. And yet the narrative train, through some tight savvy editing, always exits on the other side of the tunnel.
No #1. Invincible
Invincible takes all the superhero tropes out there and turns them on its head, with a Mystery drama that combines the anxiety of the teenage world with ultra-violence and a dark sense of humor. With a great voice cast led by Oscar nominee Steven Yeun, this animated series does justice to Robert Kirkman's superhero comics.
